Royal Game of Ur

Instructions

The Royal Game of Ur Rules:

Objective: Win the game by moving three (3) of your pieces to the end of the board.

On Your Turn:

Player rolls the dice and moves their piece that many spaces. It wilkl also protect them from being knocked out.

If you land on a circle tile you roll again.

To move your piece to the exit you need an exact roll, anything over loses your turn.
